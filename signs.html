Class A Signs

  • Also known as regulatory signs, class A signs regulate/instruct the use of roads
  • Description:They are circular in shape.

They are divided into three major sub-categories;

  • Major/Priority signs - they have priority over the others (Stop/Halt and Giveway/Yield signs)
  • Compulsory signs - they are signs to be obeyed (usually circular in shape and blue in color)
  • Prohibitory signs - they prohibit or deny something (usually circular and red in color)
